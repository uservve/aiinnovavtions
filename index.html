Types of AI

Narrow AI

Also known as Weak AI, is designed for a narrow range of tasks. Unlike humans, who can perform a variety of tasks, it excels in its particular domain but lacks general cognitive abilities.

General AI

Or Strong AI, is a theoretical form of AI that aims to mimic human intelligence. It has the ability to reason, learn, and apply knowledge across a wide range of tasksâ€”much like a human being.

Superintelligent AI

Superintelligent AI refers to AI that surpasses human intelligence across all fields, from creativity to problem-solving and beyond.
